Square Breather Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2024

At Square Breather, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This policy outlines our data handling practices.

1. Information We Collect

Square Breather collects the following types of information:

  • Heart rate data (accessed through Apple Health)
  • Usage data (e.g., frequency and duration of breathing exercises)
  • Device information (e.g., iOS version, device model)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your current heart rate during exercises
  • Provide personalized breathing exercise recommendations
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Troubleshoot and fix issues

3. Data Storage and Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal information on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data remains on your device unless you choose to share it (e.g., through Apple Health).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your personal information

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@squarebreather.com.
