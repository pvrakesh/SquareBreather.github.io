Square Breather Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start a breathing exercise?
A: To start a breathing exercise, select the desired technique from the main screen, set your preferred duration and number of cycles, then tap the "Start" button.
Q: Can I customize the duration of each breath?
A: Yes, you can adjust the duration of each phase (inhale, hold, exhale) for most exercises using the settings on the main screen before starting the exercise.
Q: How does the heart rate monitoring work?
A: Square Breather integrates with Apple Health to display your current heart rate. Ensure you've granted the necessary permissions in your device's Settings app for this feature to work.
Q: Why isn't my heart rate showing up?
A: Make sure you've granted Square Breather permission to access Health data. If issues persist, try restarting the app or your device. Remember, heart rate data depends on your device's capabilities and connected accessories.
Q: How do I turn off vibration feedback?
A: You can toggle vibration feedback on or off using the switch on the main screen before starting an exercise.
Q: Is my data safe?
A: We take your privacy seriously. Square Breather only accesses the data necessary for its functions and does not store or share your personal information. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
